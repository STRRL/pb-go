We do allow you share your code snippet with your friends. We support private share, expiring and all-encrypted storage using CHACHA20 on our server.

We reserve the rights to submit all the data to authorities we collected about you if we received a legal notice from law enforcement. We don't store any information after your paste expired or burned.

Usage

$ cmd | curl -X POST -F 'p=password' -F 'e=24' -F 'd=@-' http://DOMAIN:PORT/api/upload

"-F 'p=password'" and "-F 'e=24'" can be safely ignored.
